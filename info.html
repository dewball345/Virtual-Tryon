What is this project about?

This is a prototype of a genre in which I am making a project in - virtual tryon. This market has been rapidly expanding in the upcoming years, and I have decided to make a project because of this.

Virtual Tryon?

Virtual Tryon is the use of smartphones for testing and trying out wearables via device(and not in-person) before buying.

You can see two buttons in the home screen. The first one stops/pauses video, and the second one starts/resumes video.

Make sure that your face can be seen clearly, be, ideally 1-2 feet from the camera, and click the second button

I have not implemented a loading slider of any sort, so be aware that it will take time for the program to load. Wait 10 seconds for it to start.

When you see your face looking noticibly darker than how it normally is, and when you see a jagged line separating your face from your upper-forehead, this means that you are ready to use this

On the right of the program you will see a black bar with the names Earrings, Necklace, Nosering, and Bottu. Click these black bars to see some options

For now, click the Earring folder. You will see a bunch of options relating to earrings. Click the checkbox to see the earrings on your face.

The earrings might looks a bit off(a bit below or a bit over) your face. There are two sliders that you should pay attention to: the x-offset and y-offset slider. Changing the x-offset slider will move the earring side to side, and the y-offset slider will move the earrings up and down. Tune the position of the earrings accordingly

You can also change the scale of the earrings, as well, by changing the scale slider.

Finally, you can change the type of earrings on your face by changing the option in the dropdown menu

You can do the same things for necklaces, noserings, and bottus. I have not included rings or bangles yet, though I may in the future.
